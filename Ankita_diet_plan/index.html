Yes, adding raw banana (also known as green banana) twice a week and a ripe banana in the morning every day can be beneficial for increasing hemoglobin and ferritin levels, especially during pregnancy. Here's how each contributes to the goal:

Benefits of Raw Banana:

  • Rich in Iron: Raw banana contains a decent amount of iron, which is essential for increasing hemoglobin levels.
  • High in Resistant Starch: Raw bananas are rich in resistant starch, which has a prebiotic effect, helping in better digestion and absorption of nutrients, including iron.
  • Potassium & Fiber: Raw bananas also provide potassium, which helps in maintaining blood pressure, and fiber, which supports overall digestion.

Benefits of Ripe Banana (Daily in the Morning):

  • Iron and Vitamin C: Ripe bananas provide a small but significant amount of iron and contain vitamin C, which aids in the absorption of iron.
  • Energy & Potassium: Bananas are a good source of potassium and provide natural sugars for energy, making them an excellent way to start the day.
  • Improves Digestive Health: Bananas are rich in dietary fiber, which helps with digestion and regular bowel movements, ensuring better absorption of nutrients like iron.
