Notes for Avocado:

  • Twice a week (recommended): Adding avocado can be done with meals like the Soybean Stir-fry, Paneer Tikka, or as a side dish with Salads. Avocados contain healthy fats that aid the absorption of fat-soluble vitamins and nutrients like vitamin A, which helps iron absorption.

This format includes Avocado twice a week as per your request and is aligned with boosting hemoglobin and ferritin levels. Avocados, being rich in healthy fats, will help improve iron absorption when paired with iron

-rich meals.
