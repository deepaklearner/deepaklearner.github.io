To prepare buttermilk from curd (yogurt), follow these simple steps:

Ingredients:

  • Curd (yogurt): 2 tablespoons (or any desired amount)
  • Water or milk: 2 tablespoons (use the same amount as curd)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, take 2 tablespoons of curd (yogurt).
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of water (or milk if you prefer a richer buttermilk).
  3. Whisk or stir the mixture well until it's smooth and has a slightly thin consistency like buttermilk.
  4. The consistency should be thinner than yogurt but thicker than water.

That's it! You now have buttermilk from curd, ready to be used in your cake recipe.

Why Use Buttermilk from Curd?

  • The acidity from the curd will react with baking soda in the recipe, helping the cake rise and giving it a light, fluffy texture.
